Welcome to TaskBloomer


The tool designed to help with overwhelm, decision fatigue and workaholism.

List three concise goals you want to achieve in your day. Make these achievable within your day


Remember, be specific and intentional: there’s a character limit for a reason.

  • Task A earns you the big money - 3 points! This should be your biggest task of the day, due first or most important.
  • Task B earns you 2 points.
  • Task C earns you 1 point.

Can’t think of three? Put an intention instead!

How about: drink 6 glasses of water today // meditate for 10 minutes // make time for a walk.

Each time you complete a goal, click the ‘done’ icon and you earn points. Earn enough points and your plant will grow.
