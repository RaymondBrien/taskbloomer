Welcome to TaskBloomer


The tool designed to help with overwhelm, decision fatigue and workaholism.

List three concise goals you want to achieve in your day. Make these achievable within your day.


Remember, be specific and intentional: there’s a character limit for a reason.

  • Task A earns you the big money - 3 points! This should be your biggest task of the day, due first or most important.
  • Task B earns you 2 points.
  • Task C earns you 1 point.

Can’t think of three? Put an intention instead!


  • Drink 6 glasses of water today
  • Meditate for 10 minutes
  • Make time for a walk of at least 15 minutes

Each time you complete a goal, click the checkbox and you earn points. Earn enough points and your plant will grow.


Your goals are set to self-destruct at 8pm each night.

You change this to any time you want at the bottom of the main page.


Take a deep breath and focus on your tasks and watch your plant continue to grow!


Never-ending to-do lists and time crunches are exhausting. Focus on your three tasks, one at a time. You deserve to celebrate the little successes on the way to your big wins.

In the words of Michael Scott,

Finished them all already? Go you! Celebrate, knowing you’ve done what you set out to do today.
