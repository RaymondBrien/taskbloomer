How To Play

List three concise goals you want to achieve in your day. Make these achievable within your day.


Be specific and intentional: there’s a 25 character limit to help you be concise and precise.

Task A earns you the big money - 3 points!
This should be your biggest task of the day, due first or most important.

Task B earns you 2 points.

Task C earns you 1 point.


Can’t think of three? Put an intention instead!



Task A

Submit tax return


Task B

Meditate for 10 minutes


Task C

Make time for a walk of at least 15 minutes

Each time you complete a goal, click the checkbox to earn your points.


Collect enough points to earn NFTs. This will appear in the box under the tasks area.

Drag and drop each earned NFT into your gallery area - we've provided some cute frames for you below to hang your NFTs in.

Click the 'Your Stats' button and you will see more information about how many points you need before your next NFT and other useful tidbits for your viewing pleasure.

If you accidentally check off a goal and haven't finished it yet, simply uncheck the box. It will appear yellow to remind you that you have unchecked your goal.

Completed all your goals for the day? Click the new day button at the bottom to clear the page.

Your goals are set to self-destruct at 8pm each night!

You can change this to any time you by clicking on the 'Your Stats' button, and changing the time setting at the bottom of the page that pops up.
